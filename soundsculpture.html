soundsculpturein drama

The goal of the workshop is to help children develop a deeper understanding of language and its relationship to the world around them. By focusing on the "independent from the context of the symbolic order," the workshop intends to help children see beyond the surface level of words and symbols and gain a richer understanding of their meaning through sound sculptures!
